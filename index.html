EAROPTICA

Ghent · March–June 2027

About

Earoptica – City-Wide Festival for Media Arts is a city-wide festival unfolding across Ghent from March to June 2027. Through exhibitions, screenings, seminars, artist talks, publications and public interventions, the festival brings together artists, cultural institutions, researchers and citizens around technological innovation, ecological transition, democratic resilience and digital culture.

Earoptica starts from the understanding that technology is never neutral. It is shaped by the systems, companies and power structures that develop and govern it. While the early internet and Net.Art opened unprecedented possibilities for expression, experimentation and global connection, today's digital landscape is increasingly defined by platform control, data extraction and automated decision-making.

The festival explores how artists can reclaim technology as a civic and cultural medium. Rather than accepting digital infrastructures as fixed realities, Earoptica presents artistic practices that investigate alternative, more open and more human technological futures.

As a non-linear festival, Earoptica moves throughout the city, connecting museums, universities, alternative art spaces and the public realm. With a central presentation by Net.Art pioneers JODI.org at S.M.A.K., alongside exhibitions, lectures and interdisciplinary collaborations, Earoptica positions art not only as a reflection of society, but as an active civic force capable of shaping the systems that define our collective future.
